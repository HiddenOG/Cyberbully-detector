Cyberbullying & Terrorism Response Actions

1. Immediate Response

  • Do not respond aggressively to harmful comments.
  • Document or screenshot the offensive content.
  • If you feel threatened, contact appropriate authorities immediately.

2. Reporting

  • Use platform-specific reporting tools to flag cyberbullying or terrorist content.
  • On our platform, flagged posts/comments are highlighted automatically.
  • Encourage others to report inappropriate content responsibly.

3. Blocking & Privacy

  • Block users who repeatedly post harmful content.
  • Adjust your privacy settings to limit who can comment or interact.

4. Support & Education

  • Seek support from trusted friends, family, or counselors.
  • Educate yourself and others on recognizing harmful content.
  • Stay informed about safe online behavior and anti-terrorism awareness.
